Date Cookies Recipe

Description

Spicy cookies with chewy dates. The dates sweeten these cookies without going overboard.

Ingredients

Date Cookies

  1. 1 cup butter, softened
  2. 1 1/3 cups white sugar
  3. 3 eggs
  4. 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  5. 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  7. 2 teaspoon water
  8. 1 1/2 cups chopped pitted dates
  9. 1 cup chopped pecans
