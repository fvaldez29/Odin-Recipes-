Spice Cookies With Crystallized Ginger

Description

Crystallized ginger gives these spice cookies extra zip!

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup white sugar
  2. 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  3. 1 egg white
  4. 2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
  5. 3 tablespoons chopped crystallized ginger
  6. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  10. 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  11. 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  12. 1/3 cup granulated sugar for decoration
  13. 1/3 cup confectioners' suger for decoration
