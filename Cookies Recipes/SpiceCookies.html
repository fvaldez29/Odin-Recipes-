Spice Cookies

Description

Crystallized ginger gives these spice cookies extra zip!

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  2. 1 cup butter
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  5. 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  7. 4 cups all-purpose flour
  8. 1 cup dried currants
  9. 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  10. 1 cup milk, or as needed
